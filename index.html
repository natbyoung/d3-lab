Educational Attainment in Everett, WA

Explore educational attainment levels in neighborhoods from Everett to the southern Snohomish County/King County border. The areas shown are census tracts covering Everett, Mukilteo, Lynnwood, Edmonds, Maltby, part of Bothell, and other nearby communities.

The data reflect the highest educational attainment level for the population aged 25 and older. Five levels are included here: Less than 9th grade, high school graduate or higher, Bachelor's degree, Graduate degree, and Bachelor's degree or higher. Use the dropdown menu to select the level.

Created by Natalie Young, 2022: University of Wisconsin - Madison, Geography

Data Source: American Community Survey 2020 (Table B25077), U.S. Census Bureau

Note: The education levels shown are a selection of the full span of attainment levels and are not mutually exclusive categories.
